What do I need to play CryptoIPL ?
  • A computer or laptop running the desktop version of Chrome or Firefox
  • MetaMask, a digital wallet used specifically with web apps
  • Ether, a form of digital payment that powers CryptoIPL
What is Metamask ?
Metamask is a digital wallet ( similar to bank account in crypto world ). It comes as broswer extension. Please install and register with it. And don't forget login credentials and seed words. For more information, watch our landing page metamask video.
What is Ether ?
Ehter is digital currency, a fuel kind to Etherium technology. To make any transactions or develop decentralized applications, ehter is required. As blockchain design suggest hacking is almost imposible. So, your ethers are always safe. Ether powers the Ethereum network, which is what CryptoIPL is built on. Ether acts like any other currency; the value fluctuates with the market. You need to convert your currency (e.g. USD, INR, etc.) into ether to pay for things on our network.
How do I buy Ehters ?
  • US citizens could use Metamask to buy ethers.
  • Others need to use various exchange applications available. Transer your ehters to metamask account address
What is CryptoIpl ?
CryptoIPL is a game centered around trading, owning digital IPL teams. Each team is one-of-a-kind and 100% owned by you; it cannot be replicated, taken away, or destroyed. If someone has to own your team, he / she needs to pay you in Ehters as per our transational algorithm. ( (>= 1.5) to (<=2) rates of your current value ).
How do I make money playing CyproIPL ?
When you buy a team, you would pay team current value and when someone has to own it, he / she needs to pay you in Ehters as per our transational algorithm. ( (>= 1.5) to (<=2) rates of your current value ).
What's big deal about CryptoIPL ?
CryptoIPL is one of the India's first games to be built on blockchain technology — the same breakthrough that makes things like Bitcoin and Ethereum possible. Bitcoin and ether are cryptocurrencies, but CryptoIPL are cryptocollectibles. You can buy, sell, or trade your CryptoIPL team like a traditional collectible, secure in the knowledge that blockchain will track ownership securely.
